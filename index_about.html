Project Objectives


As a group we wanted to build and train a model that would use existing jusdicial rulings of the Supreme Court and potentially predict furture rulings as well as understand how the current Court would have ruled on historic cases.

Our data source is via Washington University Law (Cases Organized by Supreme Court Citation).
We focused our model trianing and predictions to Term, Precedent Alteration, Issue Area, Law Type, Case State Origin for justices Thomas, Ginsburg, Beryer, Roberts, Alito, Sotomayor, Kagan, and Gorsuch (1991 -2017).

Tools utilized:

Python (data cleanup and organization)
Sci-Kit Learn (model construction)
Linear SVM (model construction)
Tableau (visualizations)
